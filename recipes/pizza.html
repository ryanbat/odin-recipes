It's All About the triangles

Most reasonable people woulda gree that pizza is the best snack in existence today. In this recipe, we will
deep dive in to what makes this Italian idiom of isosceles inspiration so appetizing.

Ingredients

  • Triangles
  • Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Apply sauce to triangles
  2. Cover triangles with choice of Toppings
  3. Bake at 1200 degrees for 8 seconds
  4. Enjoy!
