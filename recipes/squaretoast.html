It's Hip to be Square

Toast is sliced bread that has been browned by radiant heat. The browning is the result of a Maillard reaction
altering the flavor of the bread and making it firmer. The firm surface is easier to spread toppings on
and the warmth can help butter reach its melting point.

Ingredients

  • Breat
  • Squares
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Ignite bread
  2. Apply bread to square mold
  3. Extinguish bread (side note: if anything else is on fire, go ahead and put it out)
  4. Apply butter and enjoy
