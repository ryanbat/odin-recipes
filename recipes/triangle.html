An Inspirational Italian Isosceles

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough (We recommended buying one premade)
  • Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Place dough on a non-stick surface
  2. Spread sauce evenly across top of dough
  3. Add cheese and other desired toppings
  4. Bake at 425 degrees for 10-15 minutes
  5. Enjoy!

    6. note: if anything is on fire, go ahead and put it out
